About Fly

Fly is a non-profit organisation that takes care of parrots and canaries. Parrots and Canaries are beautiful birds that attract a lot of attention and this will be used to our advantage as it would the way we will be able to raise money. Other countries have already had the opportunity for birds being the focal point in their animal shelters and now it is time for the United Kingdom to join. We have a loving crew who work their allotted times to ensure that the birds are thoroughly looked after for. All of this ensures us the best care for the parrots and canaries.
