Our Voluteers

The volunteers we already have working at the animal shelter are great. They take out some time out of their busy schedules and come to help out at the animal shelter. We believe this to be a very nobel thing to do as we do not have any money to spare when it comes to running this animal sanctuary. The people who come and work for us, are taught everything to do with the welfare of the birds and that means that they can do all the things our carers can do. This is a very good life skill to have and that means we believe that we are also educating people about birds as they do all the tasks that the carers at our shelter do. Here you can also find tasks are volunteers do and how to become a volunteer yourself.

Feeding

We teach volunteers how to feed the birds.

Cleansing

We teach volunteers how to clean and look after the birds.

Observations

We teach volunteers how to spot if the birds are in any danger.

Security

We teach volunteers how to save a bird from any kind of trouble.
