Reviews

We have recieved many review on the bird sanctuary. We have read all of the reviews and that is what gives us new ideas on how to improve upon what we already have. All of the reviews which have been given to us are moderated so we ensure that there will not be any comments that are inappropriate or cause any harm. We read all of the reviews that are sent to us and we upload all of them onto the website so we can get another large group of people commenting on our shelter. As you know everyone has their own opinion and if we follow them we would have one of the best shelters in the country.
